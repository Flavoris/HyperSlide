Prepare for TestFlight/App Store:

- Set General > Deployment Target iOS 16.0.
- Enable “Requires full screen” (landscape not supported in v1).
- In Info.plist:
  - NSMotionUsageDescription with friendly text if tilt is used.
- Add a simple README.md with: features, controls, settings, and known limitations.
- Ensure no unused capabilities (Game Center, iCloud) are enabled.
- Product > Archive should succeed with no warnings.
